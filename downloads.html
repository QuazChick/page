Add-ons

Weak Planks

This add-on adds 8 new wood
blocks which gradually crack as you
stand on them. The blocks will keep
their weakness when the entity steps off.

The 'Holiday Creator Features' toggle
must be active for this add-on to function.

One Player Sleep

This add-on allows players
to skip the night, even if
not all players are sleeping.

This add-on will not work on
realms as it uses experimental
features.

The 'Holiday Creator Features' toggle
must be active for this add-on to function.

Direction Viewer

This behaviour pack shows the direction
you are facing above the hotbar.
In the pack settings, you can adjust
when the direction is visible.

This behaviour pack will not work on
realms or in multiplayer games.
I will try to fix this issue
in the future.
